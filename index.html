Our story

More than lessons, a musical community

Kreutzer is a music collaboration platform bringing students together to not just learn, but join a thriving community of musicians at all levels. With opportunities to grow through recitals, ensemble work and peer support at our dedicated studios, your musical journey starts here.

Whether you're picking up an instrument for the first time or preparing for a conservatorium audition, our tailored approach meets you exactly where you are.

Questions

Frequently asked

  • We welcome students from age 5 upward, all the way through to adults. Our teaching approach is fully adapted to suit the age and learning style of each student.
  • Our studios are fully equipped so you can use instruments during lessons. For home practice we do recommend having access to an instrument, and we're happy to advise on affordable options.
  • Standard lessons are 45 minutes. For advanced students or those preparing for exams, 60-minute sessions can be arranged on request.
  • We ask for at least 24 hours notice to reschedule. Bundle package holders receive a makeup lesson guarantee — one catch-up per term at no extra cost.
  • Yes! Kreutzer hosts termly studio recitals at our dedicated venue, as well as community ensemble events throughout the year. Participation is encouraged but never compulsory.
